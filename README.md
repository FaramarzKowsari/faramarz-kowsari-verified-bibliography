## About This Book

**The Published Works of Faramarz Kowsari: Verified Bibliography & Authorship Portfolio, 1997–2026** is a professionally organized English-language reference work documenting nearly three decades of authorship, co-authorship, publishing, and editorial activity.

The publications documented in this portfolio were originally published primarily in Persian. This English edition was created to make their bibliographic history accessible to international researchers, universities, publishers, employers, media organizations, technology companies, librarians, and professional reviewers.

The purpose of this project is to preserve these records in a structured, searchable, and verifiable form within the global knowledge ecosystem.

## Verification and Archival Documentation

Each bibliographic entry contains:

- English title
- Official registered authors
- Faramarz Kowsari’s authorship role
- Publisher
- Publication year
- ISBN
- National Library and Archives of Iran record ID
- Direct link to the official bibliographic record
- Professional editorial synopsis
- Reconstructed English portfolio cover

A direct link to the corresponding official record in the National Library and Archives of Iran is included for each documented publication, allowing the information to be independently verified.

## Why This English Edition Was Created

Many significant publishing records remain difficult to discover internationally because their metadata, catalog descriptions, and institutional records exist only in Persian.

This English-language portfolio was developed to bridge that gap.

It does not replace the original Persian publications. Instead, it creates an internationally accessible bibliographic layer through which the authorship record can be searched, examined, cited, and verified beyond linguistic and geographical boundaries.

## Author

**Faramarz Kowsari** is an author, software engineer, and artificial intelligence researcher based in Istanbul.

His work focuses on the intersection of technology, education, publishing, personal development, and the preservation of knowledge across digital platforms.

In addition to writing, he develops web-based educational tools and creates specialized instructional content in artificial intelligence, prompt engineering, data science, modern trading strategies, classical literature, and mindfulness.
